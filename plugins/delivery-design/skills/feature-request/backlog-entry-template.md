# Backlog Entry Template

A single row for appending to the appropriate MoSCoW section in `docs/product/product-backlog.md`.

Append the row below to the matching section header (`## Must Have`, `## Should Have`, `## Could Have`, or `## Won't Have (this version)`). For **Won't Have**, use the two-column format with `Why Deferred` instead of `User Value` and `Scope Note`.

---

## Standard entry (Must Have / Should Have / Could Have)

| Title | User Value | Scope Note |
|---|---|---|
| [Title] | [One sentence: what the user can do and why it matters] | [Optional: constraint, dependency, or scope clarification — use — if none] |

---

## Won't Have entry

| Title | Why Deferred |
|---|---|
| [Title] | [One sentence: why this is out of scope for now] |
